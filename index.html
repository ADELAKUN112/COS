COSLAG Future Leaders Scholarship

Deadline: 13 October, 2024

The COSLAG Future Leaders Scholarship is designed to support ambitious and talented students who demonstrate leadership potential and a commitment to making a brpositive impact in their communities. This scholarship is open to undergraduate students acros all disciplines who have shown excellence in academics and leadership roles.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Must be an undergraduate student enrolled in a recognized Nigerian university.
  • Minimum GPA of 3.5 (on a 5.0 scale) or equivalent.
  • Proven leadership experience in student organizations, community service, or other leadership roles.
  • Demonstrated commitment to community development and social impact

Benefits:

  • Full tuition coverage for one academic year.
  • A leadership development workshop hosted by COSLAG.
  • Mentorship from industry leaders and COSLAG alumni.
  • Access to COSLAG’s network of professionals and community leaders.

Application Requirements:

  • Completed online application form.
  • A personal statement (500 words) detailing your leadership experience and how you plan to use your education to make a difference in your community.
  • Mentorship from industry leaders and COSLAG alumni.
  • Two letters of recommendation from academic or community leaders.

Deadline: Applications must be submitted by 13th October, 2024. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview.
